Screen: Client Tracking

This is the simple web view the client sees after clicking the tracking link.

  • The primary element is a map.
  • The map shows the client's home location and the groomer's current location.
  • An icon for the groomer would move along a projected route.
  • Below the map, a simple panel shows the groomer's status and a calculated ETA.
  • This page requires no interaction from the client. It's for viewing only.
  • When the groomer arrives, the status and ETA would update to "Your groomer has arrived!".
